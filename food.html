Understanding how agriculture supports health, economy, and sustainability through secure access to food.

тЬЕ English Explanation:

Food security means that all people, at all times, have access to sufficient, safe, and nutritious food that meets their dietary needs for a healthy and active life.

It is built on four main pillars:

  • Availability: Having enough food produced and accessible in the market.
  • Access: People being able to afford and physically reach nutritious food.
  • Utilization: Proper use of food through adequate diet, clean water, and healthcare.
  • Stability: Consistent access to food over time without disruption from crises like droughts, wars, or economic shocks.

A strong agriculture sector ensures food security by producing enough food locally, reducing dependency on imports, and stabilizing food prices. It also improves nutrition and supports health, especially for vulnerable populations.
